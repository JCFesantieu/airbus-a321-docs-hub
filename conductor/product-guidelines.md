# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
The adopted prose style for the documentation content will be Technical & Precise. This means using clear, concise, and direct language that is suitable for highly technical documentation. Ambiguity will be avoided, and terminology will be consistent and accurate to ensure that technical personnel can quickly and correctly interpret information.

## Brand Messaging
The documentation will subtly convey key messages about the Airbus brand, primarily focusing on Safety & Efficiency. Through the presentation of accurate and reliable technical data, the hub will reinforce Airbus's commitment to the highest standards of aircraft safety and operational efficiency for its customers.

## Visual Identity
The visual identity and presentation of the documentation will strictly adhere to Strict Airbus Branding guidelines. This encompasses the meticulous application of official Airbus color palettes, typography, and logo usage. The aim is to create a cohesive and recognizable brand experience that aligns with Airbus's established corporate identity.

## Content Structure
Content within individual documentation sections will prioritize Consistent Information Display. This involves the systematic use of tables, lists, and prominent call-out boxes to highlight critical information, warnings, and essential data points. This consistency aids in rapid information retrieval and reduces the potential for misinterpretation.

## Review Process
A robust review process will incorporate Version Control. All documentation updates and changes will be managed through a version control system. This ensures a complete audit trail of modifications, facilitates collaborative review, and provides mechanisms for tracking approvals and rollbacks, thereby maintaining the integrity and accuracy of the technical documentation.
